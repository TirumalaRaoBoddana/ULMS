University Leave Management System(ULMS)

A University Leave Issue Management System is a web-based application designed to facilitate the management of Leave requests for university students. The system allows users to submit Leave requests, which may include details such as the purpose of the Leave, date and time, destination, mode of transportation. These requests are then reviewed, approved, or rejected by authorized personnel within the university.
